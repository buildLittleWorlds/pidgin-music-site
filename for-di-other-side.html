                    [Verse 1]
                    I bi like owl for inside wildaness
                    Bound to ride dat northern railroad
                    My enemies dey wahala mi evriday
                    Perhaps I'll die upon dis train

                    [Chorus]
                    I be man of constant sorrow
                    My bones don almost kom out from skin
                    No friends to help me now
                    I dey die go like grass

                    [Bridge]
                    Na ashes I dey chop as food
                    Maybe your friends think I'm just a stranger
                    But I'll meet you on God's golden shore
                    Where all my wahala go end
                    
                    [Outro]
                    My life dey pass kwik-kwik like shadow
                    Fare thee well, my Kentucky home
                    Yu don pick and trow mi go down
                    But I go find peace for di other side

Some language in this song was drawn from the Wycliffe Pidgin English translation of the Bible. The Wycliffe translation can be found here.
